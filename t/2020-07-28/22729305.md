Local production saves Apple 22% import duty.<br/><br/>该公司没有降低价格，因为它也在印度销售中国制造的iPhone 11，但业内高管称，以后可能降价。在印度本地生产为苹果节省了22%的进口关税。<br/><br/>Apple makes flagship iPhone 11 at Foxconn plant in India<br/><br/>苹果在印度的富士康工厂生产旗舰机型iPhone 11<br/><br/>Apple is said to be considering a plan to make the new iPhone SE at the Wistron plant near Bengaluru, the executives said. The move also helps expand Apple’s production base beyond China at a time when Sino-US ties have soured, an executive said.<br/><br/>这一最新举动标志着苹果的制造业务日益在印度本土化，苹果渴望利用印度政府的生产激励(PLI)计划带来的好处。一位高管表示，在中美关系恶化之际，此举还有助于苹果将生产基地扩大到中国以外。<br/><br/>Apple India didn’t respond to queries.<br/><br/>苹果印度公司未回应置评请求。<br/><br/>ET had earlier reported that Apple plans to expand manufacturing in India. Supplies of the locally made device have already reached stores, said the people cited above.
